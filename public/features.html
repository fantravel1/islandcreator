Frequently Asked Questions

What tools are available?

7 tools: Sculpt (terraform), Biome (paint ecosystems), Animal (place wildlife), Build (construct structures), Zone (protect areas), Governance (set policies), and Inspect (view data overlays for vegetation, water, soil, and temperature).

How does weather affect gameplay?

Weather changes with the seasons and directly impacts water levels, vegetation growth, animal behavior, and ecosystem health. Droughts and heatwaves challenge your ecosystem, while rain helps it flourish.

What are the biomes?

5 paintable biomes: Forest (dense, cool), Grassland (moderate, temperate), Desert (arid, hot), Tundra (cold, sparse), and Jungle (lush, warm). Plus natural Ocean. Each supports different wildlife and affects your ecosystem score.

How does the governance system work?

You control conservation level, development pressure, hunting limits, and enforcement. These policies determine how settlements grow, how protected zones function, and the balance between civilization and nature.

How many achievements are there?

12 achievements spanning beginner to expert milestones: from "Island Architect" (create your first island) to "Pristine Paradise" (reach S-grade ecosystem score of 90+). Plus 5 story chapters with 15 progression goals.
