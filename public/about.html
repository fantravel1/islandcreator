Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about IslandCreator.

What is IslandCreator?

IslandCreator is a free, browser-based simulation game where you create and manage your own island ecosystem. You sculpt terrain, paint biomes, introduce wildlife, build settlements, and govern policies to maintain a thriving balance between nature and civilization.

Is it really free?

Yes, completely. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no account required. Just open the link and start playing instantly.

Do I need to download anything?

No download is needed. IslandCreator runs entirely in your web browser on both desktop and mobile devices. You can also install it as a Progressive Web App for an app-like experience.

What devices can I play on?

Any device with a modern web browser ‚Äî phones, tablets, laptops, desktops. The game is optimized for mobile with touch controls including pinch-to-zoom, two-finger panning, and double-tap zoom.

Does it save my progress?

Yes. The game auto-saves every 15 seconds to your browser's local storage. You can also manually save and load from the settings menu.

Is there combat?

No. IslandCreator is a peaceful, creation-first game with no combat, no enemies, and no fail states. It's about building, nurturing, and finding balance.
